About the Project

A Premium Gated Community in Bachupally

Candeur Eternia is crafted for modern families who want to stay rooted in Bachupally while upgrading their lifestyle. With two expansive clubhouses, a thoughtfully planned micro-climate, and contemporary architecture, Eternia delivers a sense of balance rarely found in today's crowded developments.

Luxury here isn't loud. It's intentional, comfortable, and deeply liveable — just off Miyapur Extension, with seamless access to Hyderabad's key corridors.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Candeur Eternia located?

Candeur Eternia is located in Bachupally, Hyderabad, just off Miyapur Extension. It is 5 minutes from the ORR Mallampet Exit, making it highly accessible from all parts of the city.

What apartment configurations are available?

We offer 2 BHK (1,462–1,546 sq.ft), 2.5 BHK (1,590–1,619 sq.ft), and 3 BHK (1,681–2,192 sq.ft) premium apartments in both east and west facing options.

Is Candeur Eternia RERA registered?

Yes. Candeur Eternia is RERA registered with number P02200010709. Building Permit: 001399/HMDA/00375/SWBP/MDL1/2025.

What amenities are available?

The project features 68,000+ sq.ft of amenities across two grand clubhouses — including 2 swimming pools, gymnasium, badminton & squash courts, spa, yoga room, preview theatre, restaurant, banquet hall, guest suites, pharmacy, supermarket, and much more.

What is the price range?

Please contact our sales team for the latest pricing and payment plans. We offer flexible options tailored to your needs.
