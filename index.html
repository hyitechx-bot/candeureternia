FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Candeur Eternia located?

Candeur Eternia is located in Bachupally, Hyderabad, just off Miyapur Extension. It is 5 minutes from the ORR Mallampet Exit, making it highly accessible from all parts of the city.

What apartment configurations are available?

We offer 2 BHK (1,462–1,546 sq.ft), 2.5 BHK (1,590–1,619 sq.ft), and 3 BHK (1,681–2,192 sq.ft) premium apartments in both east and west facing options.

Is Candeur Eternia RERA registered?

Yes. Candeur Eternia is RERA registered with number P02200010709. Building Permit: 001399/HMDA/00375/SWBP/MDL1/2026.

What amenities are available?

The project features 68,000+ sq.ft of amenities across two grand clubhouses — including 2 swimming pools, gymnasium, badminton & squash courts, spa, yoga room, preview theatre, restaurant, banquet hall, guest suites, pharmacy, supermarket, and much more.

What is the price of apartments at Candeur Eternia?

Candeur Eternia apartments start from ₹*. Prices vary based on configuration (2 BHK, 2.5 BHK, 3 BHK), floor, and facing. Contact our sales team for the latest pricing, offers, and flexible payment plans including the 50:50 scheme.

Specifications

Built to the Finest Detail

Every material, fitting and finish chosen with care.

Structure

Super Structure: RCC Monolithic Shear wall slab structure with 2.985M floor-to-floor height. Block work with Solid/AAC Blocks in designated areas.

Sub Structure: Basements with RCC to withstand wind and seismic loads.

External & Common Area Finishes

Exterior: Paint finish over Texture on RCC wall surfaces.

Ceiling: Paint finish over RCC / False Ceiling.

Walls: Paint finish over Texture or Putty on RCC / Cladding.

Flooring: Natural Stone / Cement / Tile flooring.

Elevators / Lifts

Passenger Lifts: Automatic elevators of reputed make in all Towers, with ARD, Surveillance, intercom connectivity and VF Drive technology.

Service Lift: One automatic elevator per Tower for Fire/Service use with ARD, Surveillance and intercom connectivity.

Doors & Windows

Main Door: Engineered wood doorframe with veneer face flush shutter in PU polish, premium hardware fittings.

Utility Door: UPVC Single Openable Casement Door.

Internal Doors: Engineered wood doorframe with Laminate face flush shutter in PU polish.

Balcony Doors: UPVC Sliding System with single glazed glass and fly mesh provision.

Windows: UPVC Fixed/Casement System with single glazed glass and fly mesh provision.

Living / Dining & Bedrooms

Walls: Premium Acrylic paint over two coats of Putty on RCC/Plaster Surface.

Ceiling: False Ceiling cornice finished with premium acrylic paint.

Flooring: Vitrified tiles of reputed make.

HVAC: Provision for Drain & Power and Copper pipe routing.

TV: Wall-mounted TV provision with LAN Points in all living areas and Master Bedroom.

Kitchen

No Kitchen Counters provided; plumbing provision for Sink included.

Walls: Premium Acrylic paint over Putty. Flooring: Vitrified tiles.

RO: Electrical and Plumbing Provision. Exhaust/Chimney: Electrical & Space provision.

Electrical & Infrastructure

6A sockets in all rooms. Provision for Hob, Chimney, Refrigerator, Microwave, Mixer/Grinder in Kitchen.

Power Backup: 100% DG Backup with auto changeover (excluding EV charging).

EV Charging: Provision for Common EV Charging Stations in basements.

Telecom: Telephone in Living Room, Intercom to security, LAN at all TV locations.

Water, Security & Safety

Water: Treated domestic supply via Hydro Pneumatic system. SBR-type STP in basements. Rainwater harvesting for domestic use.

Security: Centralised billing for Electricity, Water & LPG. CCTV in basements, common areas, tower entries and all elevators.

Fire Safety: Fire hydrant & sprinkler system on all floors. Fire alarm & PA system. Fire command centre at ground level.

Gas: Centralised Gas bank supply with pre-paid meters or city piped gas.
